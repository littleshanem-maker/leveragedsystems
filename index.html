About

Built by someone who's been on your side of the desk.

Leveraged Systems is a solo construction technology studio. One founder. Purpose-built tools for Australian subcontractors.

I spent 15 years as a Project Manager on Tier 1 and Tier 2 infrastructure in Victoria — West Gate Tunnel, major civil and services packages. I know what it looks like when a subcontractor does $80,000 in legitimate variation work and walks away with nothing because the paperwork wasn't right.

I built Variation Shield because I was tired of watching good operators get burned by process failures, not performance failures. The work was done. The agreement wasn't captured. That's a fixable problem.

These tools aren't built by a software team that's never worn PPE. They're built from the field up — for the way construction actually works, not the way it's supposed to work on paper.

Shane Little — Founder, Leveraged Systems
Geelong, Victoria
