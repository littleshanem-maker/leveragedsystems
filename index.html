Construction Workflow & Commercial Control Audit

A practical review of how your business captures, tracks and manages site information, variations, instructions, evidence and commercial admin.

Most contractors do not lose control because they lack more software.
They lose control because site instructions, photos, RFIs, variations and commercial admin are scattered across emails, spreadsheets, phones and memory.

The audit identifies where information is being lost, where commercial risk is building, and where better workflows or practical AI can improve control without adding unnecessary complexity.

Why we exist

Built from firsthand experience

Leveraged Systems was founded by a former Tier 1 project manager with 15 years reviewing, processing and deciding variation claims from subcontractors.

We saw the same thing every week. Skilled tradespeople doing legitimate extra work. Good claims rejected because the paperwork wasn't there. Not because the work wasn't done - because the evidence wasn't documented properly.

Missing evidence doesn't just lose you the variation. It loses you the argument. By the time you're in the final account meeting, the leverage is already gone.

Leveraged Systems exists to stop commercial value leaking through memory, spreadsheets and disconnected admin.
